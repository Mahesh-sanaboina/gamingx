# How to Build a Gaming Setup from Zero to 100% (Beginner Guide)

Welcome to the GamingX Beginner Guide! This document will walk you through the essentials of building your first professional gaming station.

## 1. Gaming PC Basics
- CPU: The brain of your system. Look for 6 cores or more.
- GPU: Essential for gaming. NVIDIA RTX 4060 or AMD RX 7600 are great starters.
- RAM: 16GB is the modern standard for gaming.

## 2. Monitor Setup
- Refresh Rate: Aim for at least 144Hz for a smooth experience.
- Resolution: 1080p is perfect for beginners.

## 3. Keyboard & Mouse Setup
- Mechanical Keyboards: Offer better feedback and durability.
- Lightweight Mouse: Essential for precision in FPS games.

## 4. Gaming Accessories
- Desk Mat: Large surface for mouse movement.
- Headset: Comfort is key for long sessions.

## 5. RGB Setup Ideas
- Use soft, consistent colors (Cyan, Purple, or White).
- LED strips behind the desk create an ethereal glow.

## 6. Budget Gaming Setup
- Focus on performance first, aesthetics later.
- Buy used monitors to save for a better GPU.

## 7. Beginner Streaming Setup
- Use OBS Studio for broadcasting.
- A basic 1080p webcam and a clip-on mic are enough to start.
